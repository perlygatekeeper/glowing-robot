RPN Calculator
Release Notes
Version 3.6.1
=============

Release Date
------------
June 2026


Overview
--------
Version 3.6.1 introduces combinatorics and probability
functions to the calculator.

These functions support factorials, permutations,
combinations, and common binomial distribution calculations.

This release fills a long-standing gap in the calculator's
mathematical capabilities and provides a foundation for
future probability and statistics enhancements.


New Command Type
----------------

combinatorics

The following commands have been added:

    factorial
    npr
    ncr
    binom
    binompdf
    binomcdf


Factorials
----------

New command:

    factorial

Alias:

    fact

Examples:

    5 factorial

Result:

    120

    0 factorial

Result:

    1


Permutations
------------

New command:

    npr

Alias:

    permute

Examples:

    4 2 npr

Result:

    12

Explanation:

    4P2 = 4 × 3 = 12


Combinations
------------

New command:

    ncr

Aliases:

    binom
    comb

Examples:

    4 2 ncr

Result:

    6

Explanation:

    4C2 = 6

Equivalent forms:

    4 2 binom

    4 2 comb


Binomial Probability
--------------------

New command:

    binompdf

Computes the probability of obtaining exactly k
successes in n independent Bernoulli trials.

Example:

    10 3 0.5 binompdf

Result:

    0.1171875


Cumulative Binomial Probability
-------------------------------

New command:

    binomcdf

Computes the probability of obtaining k or fewer
successes in n independent Bernoulli trials.

Example:

    10 3 0.5 binomcdf

Result:

    0.171875


Error Handling
--------------

All combinatorics commands validate their operands.

Invalid operands:

    non-numeric values
    negative values
    non-integer values
    invalid probabilities

are rejected without modifying stack contents.

Examples:

    'hello factorial

    4 'two ncr

    10 3 2 binompdf

The stack remains unchanged after the error.
