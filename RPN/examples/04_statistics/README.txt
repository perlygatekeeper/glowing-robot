04 Statistics
=============

Recipes
-------

* Mean: Demonstrates average computation.
* Median: Demonstrates sorting and selection.
* Mode: Demonstrates counting repeated values.
* Histogram: Demonstrates grouping and counting.
* Normalize a Data Set: Demonstrates scaling values consistently.
* Z-Scores: Demonstrates standardization.
* Five-Number Summary: Demonstrates descriptive statistics.
* Outlier Detection: Demonstrates filtering unusual values.
