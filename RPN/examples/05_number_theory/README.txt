05 Number Theory
================

Recipes
-------

* Sieve of Eratosthenes: Demonstrates filtering candidates.
* Prime Factorization: Demonstrates repeated division and factor extraction.
* Euler's Totient: Demonstrates number-theory composition.
* Modular Exponentiation: Demonstrates efficient repeated operations.
* Perfect Numbers: Demonstrates divisor sums.
* Fibonacci Sequence: Demonstrates recurrence.
